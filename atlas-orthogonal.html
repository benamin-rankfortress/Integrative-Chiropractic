Frequently Asked Questions

What is Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic?
Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic is a specialized form of chiropractic care that utilizes precise, gentle adjustments to the top vertebra in the spine to restore balance and proper alignment.
How does Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic work?
Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic works by using precision instruments to identify misalignments in the spine and then gently adjusting the first vertebrae at the base of the skull (the atlas) so that it is properly aligned with the rest of the spine. This helps restore balance and reduce inflammation, pain, and tension throughout the body.
What are the benefits of Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic?
The benefits of Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic include improved posture, increased range of motion, reduced pain and discomfort, improved overall health and wellness, better sleep quality, reduced stress levels, improved mental clarity, enhanced athletic performance, improved digestion and overall immune system function.
Who can benefit from Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic?
Anyone who has experienced physical or emotional trauma or wants to improve their overall health can benefit from Atlas Orthogonal Chiropractic care. It can be used to treat chronic pain conditions such as migraines or neck/back problems as well as many other conditions including fibromyalgia, sciatica, scoliosis, carpal tunnel syndrome and more.
